frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is solar inverter?
A solar inverter is a device that convert the (DC) direct current generated by the solar panels into alternating current (AC) that can be used to power household appliances and the electrical grid.
How does a solar inverter work?
Solar inverters work by converting the DC power generated by solar panels into AC power, which is the type of electricity used in homes and businesses. This enables the utilization of solar energy for various applications.
How many solar panels can an inverter handle?
The number of solar panels you can connect to your inverter is identified by its wattage rating.
What are the benefits of solar?
Reduced electricity bills, Lower carbon emissions, No running costs, Low maintenance, 25 years + life expectancy
